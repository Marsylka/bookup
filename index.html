Public Discussion "Women's Perspective on IT: Possibilities of Self-Realization and Combating Stereotypes" within the Case of Yes Project

We invite you to a public discussion with the participation of Maria Golubieva, Darya Smirnova and Natalia Ilchenko, "Women's Perspective on IT: Possibilities of Self-Realization and Combating Stereotypes", to be held on November 7 in the framework of the Case E project. The start is at 18:30. Moderator - Galina Oliynyk. Let's talk about: How to get into IT? How to stay there and what to expect? Gender discrimination in the workplace: how to overcome dragon stereotypes? Careers and flexible schedules: Why is IT a good choice for women? How to improve your professional level? “It is never too late to become an outcast” is the motivation for choosing a profession. Women Who Code Kyiv project participants will take part in the discussion: Maria Golubev. She started working in IT with GameDev, working in a small popappfactory studio where she created children's games for iOS / macOS / Android. Currently engaged in iOS mobile app development. Daria Smirnova. Two years of industry experience. He works as a back-end developer at RetargetApp, an official Facebook marketing partner in Ukraine. Natalia Ilchenko. Director of the Women Who Code Kyiv platform. Galina Oliynyk. Natural Language Processing Specialist at 1touch.io, an advanced platform for client data management. Women Who Code Kyiv is a local platform of international initiative for women programmers. The project aims to educate, support, share experiences, motivate and promote programming among women. The Case of Yes is a project of Bookstores of Yes, through which our guests have the opportunity to get acquainted with the creators, founders and authors of successful Ukrainian cases. The project affects a wide range of areas. We believe and know that we are who and what to be proud of.
